Our Specializations

• Licence (MI)

This Double Degree offers solid and balanced training in computer science and mathematics, to then prepare in excellent conditions for a Master's degree in one of the two disciplines or interfaces. It also allows you to prepare for entry into major schools. Mathematics and IT are at the heart of the digital transition which impacts and transforms all activities and professions. Students are trained to model problems, seek and find solutions, and code them effectively. They acquire the disciplinary foundations and approach the fields of machine learning and data science, the foundations of artificial intelligence.

The training places a large emphasis on interdisciplinary projects which allow you to develop curiosity and creativity and to learn about research and teamwork.

The emphasis is placed on mastery of written and oral scientific communication, in French and English.

• Licence (ST)

A License Science et Technologie, also known as a Bachelor of Science and Technology, is a three-year undergraduate university program in France focused on scientific fields. It can be broadly categorized into two types:

- Multidisciplinary: This type provides a foundation in a wide range of scientific disciplines, including mathematics, physics, biology, chemistry, computer science, and engineering. It's suitable for students from scientific baccalaureate backgrounds (STi2D, STAV, STL) who might need extra support transitioning to university-level studies. The aim is to prepare them for further scientific studies in a master's program or engineering school.
- Specialized: This type focuses on a particular scientific discipline, such as telecommunications, mechanics, or digital technology. The specific area of focus and career paths will vary depending on the university offering the program.

Here are some additional points to consider:

- The program strengthens the student's scientific approach, including how science is learned, managed, and how it interacts with society.
- Universities have the freedom to design their own course structures within the broader License Science and Technology framework.
- This means the specific curriculum may differ depending on the institution.
- If you're interested in learning more about a specific License Science et Technologie program, it's best to consult the website of the university you're interested in attending.

• Licence (SNV)

Here's what you can expect from a Licence SNV program:

- Focus: It provides a broad foundation in natural sciences like biology, chemistry, physics, ecology, and life sciences like biochemistry, microbiology, and genetics.
- Specializations: Some universities might offer specializations within the program, such as Biology, Agronomy, Food Science, or Biotechnology.
- Curriculum: The common core curriculum likely covers general science courses, but universities have the flexibility to design specific programs with a focus on their strengths and resources.
- Career Paths: This degree prepares students for further studies in Master's programs or specialized schools in various natural and life science fields. It can also qualify graduates for entry-level positions in laboratories, environmental agencies, or the food industry (depending on the specific program).

For a more detailed understanding of the program content and specializations offered, it's advisable to visit the website of the particular Algerian university you're interested in.
